WELCOME TO THE FOOTBALL PLAYER PREDICTION GAME

Let's understand the rules first

You will be shown images and names of 6 random players who are in FIFA20.

Next you will be given 5 hints, one by one.
You can guess the player at any point of time by clicking on the image of that player.

If you guess it wrong then you will be thrown back to the welcome page.
If you guess it right, you will be given subsequent points.

60 - Answering on first hint
50 - Answering on second hint
40 - Answering on third hint
20 - Answering on fourth hint
10 - Answering on last hint
0 - For not answering(what else do you expect)
